Space Intelligence serves as the central hub for your entire robot fleet:

- **Real-time visibility** into all robot operations across locations
- **Multi-vendor support** — manage robots from different manufacturers on one screen
- **Enterprise integration** — connect with WMS, ERP, MES, and other systems
- **Multi-vehicle orchestration** — coordinate robots, personnel, and non-robotic assets
